# Services

At **Burden Digital Solutions (BDS)**, every project begins by understanding the business problem before selecting the technology.

Our services are organized into two areas:

- Strategy & Planning
- Design & Development

---

# Strategy & Planning

Helping businesses understand the problem before building the solution.

## Discovery & Solution Planning

Understand business goals, identify pain points, define project objectives, and recommend the best technical solution.

## Requirements Gathering

Document functional requirements, business needs, project scope, and success criteria.

## User Research

Understand target users through interviews, observations, and research to ensure solutions meet real customer needs.

## UI / UX Design

Plan intuitive user experiences through user flows, information architecture, and usability best practices.

## Wireframing & Prototyping

Create low- and high-fidelity prototypes to validate ideas before development begins.

---

# Design & Development

Building modern software solutions that solve business problems.

## Website Development

Professional, responsive websites for businesses, organizations, and personal brands.

## Website Redesign

Modernize existing websites to improve usability, accessibility, and performance.

## Dashboard Development

Develop custom dashboards that organize and visualize business data for internal teams.

## Custom Web Applications

Build tailored web applications that improve workflows, automate processes, and support business operations.

## Deployment & Support

Deploy applications securely and provide ongoing updates, maintenance, and support.
