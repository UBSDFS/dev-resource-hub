# Client Process

This directory documents my complete client workflow from initial contact through project delivery.

The goal is to provide a repeatable process that ensures every client receives a consistent, professional experience while keeping projects organized and well documented.

---

## Workflow

1. Client Intake
2. Discovery Call
3. Project Scope
4. Proposal
5. Contract
6. Content Collection
7. Design Approval
8. Project Kickoff
9. Development
10. Launch
11. Client Handoff

---

## Philosophy

Every successful project begins with understanding the client's business before writing code.

The objective is not simply to build a website, but to deliver a solution that solves a business problem.

Planning, communication, and documentation are as important as development.
