# Discovery Call

## Purpose

Understand the client's business, goals, users, pain points, and project expectations before creating a proposal or writing code.

## When to Use

Use this after the client intake form has been completed and before creating the project scope or proposal.

## Owner

Project Lead / Software Consultant

## Inputs

- Client intake notes
- Business information
- Existing website or product links
- Client goals
- Initial budget and timeline expectations

## Outputs

- Clear understanding of the business problem
- List of required features
- List of nice-to-have features
- Initial project risks
- Next steps for project scope

---

## Discovery Questions

### Business Goals

- What does your business do?
- What problem are you trying to solve with this website or product?
- What would make this project successful?
- What is the main action you want users to take?

### Target Users

- Who is the primary audience?
- What do they need when they visit the site?
- What problems are they trying to solve?
- Are there different types of users?

### Current Situation

- Do you currently have a website?
- What is working well?
- What is not working well?
- What feedback have customers given you?

### Features

- What features are required?
- What features would be nice to have?
- Do you need forms, booking, payments, login, admin access, or a dashboard?
- Do you need to manage content yourself?

### Design Direction

- Are there websites you like?
- Are there websites you dislike?
- Do you already have a logo, brand colors, fonts, or photos?
- What feeling should the website give users?

### Content

- Who will provide the written content?
- Who will provide images or videos?
- Do you need help organizing content?
- Are there existing documents, brochures, or social media pages I should review?

### Timeline

- Is there a specific launch date?
- Are there any major events or deadlines connected to this project?
- How quickly can feedback be provided during the project?

### Budget

- Is there an approved budget range?
- Are there future phases planned?
- Is ongoing maintenance needed after launch?
